Welcome to SuperCam!
_______________________________________
This android application will manage your wardrobe for you. Worried about what to wear next saturday? Don't be! This wardrobe manager will ensure you don't wear the same thing over and over again. It'll help you pick your clothes. Who needs friends when you have this?

* Supports automatic color detection
* Supports backup to google drive
* Supports organisation of clothes base on type (top, skirt, formal, party wear etc.) and color.
* Has a laundry feature which lets you add clothes from your wardrobe to the laundry, and back to your wardrobe when they're ready to be worn again. Ensures you know which dresses you can wear and which you can't.
* Changes the way your dress, now and forever.
